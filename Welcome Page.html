Data Storage
Us at TextIt Now don't save any unnecessary data and we are focusing more and more everyday to encrypt your data. We don't store any passwords you sign in with your email and that is the only thing linking you to your account.
Connecting
Knowing who you are connecting with is a very important part of TextIt Now we really want the students that use TextIt Now to know 100% who they are talking to, so we take that very seriously.
Security
For all the districts that we allow TextIt Now is exclusive to only the students of that district. We stop other students from talking with other students/people outside their district so it is secure and safe for the students to ensure that the students know who they are chatting with.

About Our Workers

Our workers are devoted to helping make sure the workflow is efficient and safe for all students to work and enjoy their time on TextIt Now.

Accessibility

We tried to make our web app as accessible as possible whereas some other messaging apps favor some types of phones, to do this we made the website so you can access your account on any device with a search engine.
